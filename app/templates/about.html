AndroidSnippets is a community driven website for finding, exploring, sharing and improving source code snippets for Android.

Improving Snippets

Every user can edit snippets (by clicking on the "edit" button on the right side), and submit improvements for the community to review. Edits are visible on the snippet's page and can be upvoted and downvoted by registered users. Once an edit reaches a sum of +5, it is used as the default version. For every future upvote on the snippet, every author of an 'merged' edit as well as the original author gain one reputation point.

Reputation Points

Reputation points are a rough indicator of a user's contributions to the snippet collection. They can be gained by:

  1. submitting a snippet: +1
  2. every upvote on your snippet: +1
  3. every upvote on your edit of a snippet: +1
  4. every downvote on your edit of a snippet: -1
  5. if your edit of a snippet is merged: +2

Licenses

Every submitter of a code snippet agrees to the terms of service, which allow everyone to reuse and modify the snippet without any current or future restrictions.

The source code of this website is hosted on github and released under the AGPL.
